## Getting Started

### What is Between Us?

Between Us is a private couples app for daily prompts, shared reveals, journals, date planning, memories, signals, and recaps.

Between Us is for thriving couples only. It is not a therapy app, relationship repair tool, crisis service, or substitute for professional support.

### Who is Between Us for?

Between Us is for adults in relationships who already feel safe and respected and want to deepen connection.

It is not appropriate for couples navigating abuse, coercion, serious trust breakdowns, active crisis, or separation decisions. If your relationship needs professional support, please contact a qualified therapist, counselor, attorney, emergency service, or local support organization.

### Is Between Us inclusive?

Yes. Between Us is designed to be inclusive of adults across orientations, identities, relationship structures, cultures, and relationship lengths. The app does not require you to provide sexual orientation as an account field.

### How much does it cost?

- **Free version:** 1 guided prompt answer per day, weekly previews across Heat levels 1-5, limited date and intimacy position previews, 2 full date flows per week, partner linking, and supported data export
- **Premium Monthly:** fallback display price $7.99/month
- **Premium Yearly:** fallback display price $49.99/year

Actual prices, trials, offers, taxes, and currency are shown by the App Store or RevenueCat purchase flow and may vary by region or store configuration.

### How do I get started?

1. Create your account
2. Add your names and preferences
3. Link with your partner, if you want the shared experience
4. Answer prompts, plan dates, and save moments

## Account & Setup

### Do I need my partner to use the app?

No. You can use Between Us solo for prompts, notes, journaling, reflection, and date ideas. Linking with a partner unlocks the shared reveal and couple-space experience.

### How do I link with my partner?

1. Both partners create accounts
2. One partner generates an invite code or QR code
3. The other partner scans the QR code or enters the code
4. Both accounts are linked into one couple space

### Can I unlink from my partner?

Yes. Either partner can unlink. Unlinking dissolves the shared couple connection, stops future shared syncing, and leaves your separate account in place.

### Can I link with multiple partners?

Not at the same time. Between Us currently supports one linked partner at a time.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data private?

Between Us protects data with account authentication, Supabase row-level security, HTTPS/TLS, provider-side security controls, app access controls, and optional app lock on your device. We do not sell your personal data and do not use advertising networks.

### Is my content end-to-end encrypted?

No. The active storage architecture does not currently provide end-to-end encryption for core synced content. Journals, prompt answers, memories, check-ins, vibes, calendar events, date plans, and media attachments are protected by account access controls and transport security, but they are not currently encrypted end-to-end before upload.

### Can Between Us read my journal entries?

Journal entries are not currently end-to-end encrypted. We restrict access through authentication, row-level security, internal access controls, and service-provider protections, but we cannot accurately claim that our systems are technically unable to access core synced content.

### Can my partner see everything I write?

Between Us is built around shared couple-space content. Your linked partner may be able to access content stored in that shared couple space according to the feature flow. Prompt answers are designed to reveal through the shared reveal experience. App-lock settings, payment info, password credentials, and biometric data are not shared with your partner.

### What happens if we break up?

You can unlink from your partner. Unlinking dissolves the shared couple connection and stops future shared syncing. You can also delete your account if you want your account and supported data removed according to the deletion flow.

### Does the app collect biometric data?

No. Face ID and Touch ID are handled by your device. Between Us receives only a success/failure result from the device authentication prompt and does not collect, store, or transmit biometric templates.

### What data may stay only on my device?

Some data is cached on your device, such as cached synced content, offline write queues, notification ids, app-lock enabled state, and cache-only preference history. Some settings, including energy level, relationship climate, and soft boundaries, may also sync to your profile so they can be restored across devices.

### What third-party services do you use?

Between Us uses Supabase for authentication, database, storage, backend functions, and realtime features; RevenueCat for subscriptions; Sentry for crash reporting, performance monitoring, limited session replay, and optional feedback; Expo for notifications and app services; and Apple for App Store distribution and payment processing.

## Features & Usage

### What are Heat Levels?

Heat levels help you choose the intensity of prompts:

- **Heat 1:** Emotional connection
- **Heat 2:** Flirty and romantic
- **Heat 3:** Sensual
- **Heat 4:** Steamy
- **Heat 5:** Explicit

Free users can preview Heat levels 1-5. Premium opens more of the released prompt, date, and intimacy position catalog each week.

### How do prompts work?

1. Open the app and view a prompt
2. Write your answer
3. If linked, your partner can answer too
4. Use the reveal flow to see answers together
5. Save meaningful moments to your archive

### Can I skip prompts I don't like?

Yes. You can skip prompts, refresh where available, lower heat level, use soft boundaries, or hide categories.

### How are prompts personalized?

The app may use relationship duration, heat level, energy level, relationship climate, soft boundaries, prompt engagement, and local preference signals to shape recommendations. Some personalization happens locally and some synced preferences may be stored in your profile for continuity.

### What are Vibe Signals and Moment Signals?

Vibe Signals and Moment Signals are quick ways to share mood, presence, or "thinking of you" moments with your linked partner when connectivity allows.

### What is Reveal Together?

Reveal Together is the flow where partners answer and then reveal prompt responses in a shared moment.

### What are Soft Boundaries?

Soft Boundaries let you hide or pause content categories you do not want to see. They are designed as your own content controls. Some related settings may sync to your profile for backup or cross-device continuity.

### What is the Energy Matcher?

Energy Matcher lets you set a current energy level so the app can suggest prompts and dates that better match the moment.

### What is the Inside Jokes vault?

The Inside Jokes vault is a place to save relationship language, nicknames, references, and special phrases. Some inside-joke data may be local to the device depending on the feature path.

### What is Year Reflection?

Year Reflection and recap features summarize relationship moments from supported app data such as journals, dates, memories, answers, and signals.

### What is Surprise Tonight?

Surprise Tonight suggests spontaneous date or connection ideas based on available preferences and context.

### Can I lock the app with Face ID, Touch ID, or PIN?

Yes. You can enable app lock in Settings > Privacy & Security. App lock controls access on the device. It does not mean synced server content is end-to-end encrypted.

## Technical Support

### The app isn't working. What should I do?

1. Force close and reopen the app
2. Check your internet connection
3. Check for app updates
4. Restart your device
5. Contact support if the issue continues

### My partner and I aren't syncing. Help!

Check that:

- Both partners have internet access
- Both partners are signed in
- Your accounts are still linked
- You are using the latest available app version
- You have not recently unlinked or switched accounts

### I forgot my password. What can I do?

Use the password recovery flow to request a 6-digit recovery code by email and set a new password. If Magic Link sign-in is available in your build, you can use it for passwordless access.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Tap Delete Account
3. Type DELETE when prompted
4. Confirm deletion

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone once completed. Active account data is removed according to the app's deletion flow and Privacy Policy. Backup copies may persist for a limited period before routine purge.

### I'm not receiving notifications. Why?

Check that:

- Notifications are enabled in app settings
- Notifications are allowed in device settings
- Do Not Disturb or Focus modes are not blocking notifications
- Your device has network access

### Can I use Between Us on multiple devices?

Yes, you can sign in on multiple devices. Synced data can appear across devices after sign-in and sync. Pending offline changes and cache-only preferences may not be recoverable on another device.

## Safety & Wellbeing

### Is Between Us a substitute for therapy?

No. Between Us is not therapy, counseling, medical care, legal advice, crisis support, or relationship repair.

### When should we NOT use Between Us?

Do not rely on Between Us for serious relationship concerns, abuse, coercion, threats, infidelity recovery, separation decisions, or crisis situations. Seek qualified help.

### What if prompts trigger difficult emotions?

Skip the prompt, lower the heat level, use soft boundaries, take a break, and seek professional support if needed.

### What if I experience harassment or feel unsafe?

Unlink from your partner if it is safe to do so, contact support, and reach out to appropriate safety resources. If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

## Miscellaneous

### Does Between Us work offline?

Between Us supports offline continuity for some actions through local cache and an offline write queue. Supabase is the canonical source of truth when online. Pending local writes sync when connectivity and account state allow.

### What languages do you support?

Currently, Between Us is English-first.

### Can I export my data?

Yes. Supported export data may include journals, prompt answers, memories, check-ins, vibes, calendar events, date plans, and account details.

Exports are prepared as a plaintext JSON file for the system share sheet. Between Us removes the temporary export file from app storage after the export flow completes. Any copy you save or share from the share sheet is controlled by the destination you choose.
