# Copy Wording Guide

Use this guide for user-facing copy in product screens, paywalls, onboarding, settings, and legal summaries.

## Core Rule

Do not promise behavior the app cannot guarantee in every case.

Prefer wording that reflects:
- connectivity-dependent delivery
- linked-account requirements
- device-only versus synced data
- backup-based deletion timelines

## Preferred Phrases

Use:
- "when connected"
- "can receive shared access"
- "linked partner"
- "encrypted sync"
- "removed from active systems promptly"
- "backup purge may take up to 90 days"
- "device-only"
- "private on this device"
- "sensitive content"

## Avoid Phrases

Avoid unless implementation guarantees them:
- "real-time"
- "instantly"
- "immediately"
- "always"
- "never" for operational behavior
- "both partners included"
- "automatically gets access"
- "removed from both accounts"
- "deleted immediately"
- "keys stay on-device"
- "all content is end-to-end encrypted"

## Approved Patterns

### Heartbeat / Pulse

Prefer:
- "send a tactile pulse"
- "heartbeat signal"
- "device should pulse now"

Avoid:
- "feel your partner's heartbeat"
- "their heartbeat is synced"

### Sync / Delivery

Prefer:
- "synced when connected"
- "sends a push when available"
- "shared quickly when both devices are connected"

Avoid:
- "instant sync"
- "real-time delivery"
- "push immediately"

### Premium Access

Prefer:
- "one subscription can share premium access with your linked partner"
- "linked partners can share premium access"

Avoid:
- "both partners included"
- "your partner automatically gets access"

### Privacy / Encryption

Prefer:
- "shared synced content is encrypted before upload"
- "device-only content stays on your phone"
- "some metadata is protected but not end-to-end encrypted"

Avoid:
- "all user-created content is end-to-end encrypted"
- "all data is encrypted at rest on our servers" when referring to client-side secrets

### Deletion / Unlinking

Prefer:
- "unlinking stops new sharing"
- "removed from active systems promptly"
- "backup purge may take up to 90 days"

Avoid:
- "nothing after you unlink"
- "shared content is removed from both accounts"
- "deleted immediately"

## Review Checklist

Before shipping copy, check:
- Does this claim require network connectivity?
- Does this assume the couple is already linked?
- Is this content device-only or synced?
- Is this describing encryption accurately?
- Does this imply a timing guarantee?
- Does this imply deletion is instantaneous?
