## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is Between Us?**
A private couples app for deepening your connection through daily prompts, shared journaling, memories, signals, and date nights. Designed for thriving couples only.
 
**Do I need my partner to use it?**
No. You can use it solo for journaling and reflection. Linking with your partner unlocks shared features.

**How do I link with my partner?**
One partner generates an invite code or QR code in the app. The other scans or enters it. Both confirm. You're connected.

**Is my data private?**
Between Us protects synced content with account access controls, row-level security, secure transport, and provider-side policies. Core synced content is not currently end-to-end encrypted. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.

**How much does it cost?**
Free version available. Premium: $7.99/month or $49.99/year. One subscription can share premium access with your linked partner after entitlement sync completes.
 
**How do I delete my account?**
In the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Between Us → Cancel.

## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 24, 2026**

Your privacy matters. Between Us protects synced content with authentication, row-level security, secure transport, and provider-side controls. Core synced content is not currently end-to-end encrypted. We never sell your data or share your relationship content with advertisers.

### Information We Collect

**Account Information:** Email address, display name, partner names, relationship start date (optional).

**Content You Create:** Journal entries, prompt responses, shared memories, milestones, check-ins, vibes, moment signals, inside jokes, nicknames, heat level preferences, calendar events, date plans, media attachments you choose to add, and relationship duration.

**Automatically Collected:** Device type and model, OS version, app version, device identifiers (for authentication), features used, error logs, and crash reports.

**We Never Collect:** Sexual orientation, precise location, contact lists, photos unless you explicitly share them, biometric templates, or your credit card number.

### How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide personalized prompts, sync data between you and your partner, save your entries, send notifications you opt into, fix bugs, improve performance, and respond to support requests.

### Data Security

Core synced content is stored through Supabase and protected by account authentication, row-level security, HTTPS/TLS, provider-side security controls, and app/backend access controls. Core synced content is not currently encrypted end-to-end before upload.

Device data is limited to cache, pending offline writes, notification ids, display preferences, and app-lock enabled state. App lock helps protect access on your device but does not make synced server content end-to-end encrypted.

### Data Sharing

**With Your Partner:** Content you choose to share is visible to your linked partner. You control what you share and when. Unlinking stops new data sharing.

**With Service Providers:**
- Supabase — authentication, database, storage, backend functions, and realtime features
- RevenueCat — subscription management (no personal content shared)
- Sentry — crash reporting, performance monitoring, limited session replay, and optional feedback
- Apple — payment processing (we never see your payment details)

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies and may collect data independently as needed to provide their services.

**We Never Share With:** Advertisers, data brokers, marketing companies, social media platforms, or third-party apps.

We may disclose information if required by valid court orders, subpoenas, or legal process. We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

### Your Rights

You can view, export, edit, or delete supported data within the app. You can delete your account in Settings → Account → Delete Account. Active account data is removed according to the in-app deletion flow. Backup copies may persist for a limited period before routine purge.

### Children's Privacy

Between Us is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

### Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
