## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 26, 2026**

Your privacy is our top priority. Between Us encrypts your intimate content before it ever leaves your device, never sells your data, and never shares your content with third parties.

### Information We Collect

**Account Information:** Email address, display name, partner names, relationship start date (optional).

**Content You Create:** Journal entries, prompt responses, love notes, shared memories, milestones, inside jokes, nicknames, heat level preferences, calendar events, vibe signal selections, and relationship duration.

**Automatically Collected:** Device type and model, OS version, app version, device identifiers (for authentication), features used, error logs, and crash reports.

**We Never Collect:** Sexual orientation, precise location, contact lists, photos (unless you explicitly share), biometric data, or financial information.

### How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide personalized prompts, sync data between you and your partner, save your entries, send notifications you opt into, fix bugs, improve performance, and respond to support requests.

### Data Security and Encryption

Shared synced content is protected with end-to-end encryption. Journal entries, prompt responses, memories, and love notes are encrypted on your device before upload using XSalsa20-Poly1305 (NaCl secretbox). Encryption keys are derived via X25519 key exchange during partner pairing and never stored on our servers.

Additional local data is encrypted with a device-specific key stored in your device's secure Keychain. Some metadata (timestamps, event types, mood labels) is stored without end-to-end encryption to support features like filtering and sorting — this metadata is still protected by TLS in transit and access controls at rest.

### Data Sharing

**With Your Partner:** Content you choose to share is visible to your linked partner. You control what you share and when. Unlinking stops new data sharing.

**With Service Providers:**
- Supabase — cloud hosting (your content is end-to-end encrypted)
- RevenueCat — subscription management (no personal content shared)
- Sentry — crash reporting and performance monitoring
- Apple — payment processing (we never see your payment details)

All providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

**We Never Share With:** Advertisers, data brokers, marketing companies, social media platforms, or third-party apps.

We may disclose information if required by valid court orders, subpoenas, or legal process. We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

### Your Rights

You can view, export, edit, or delete your data at any time within the app. You can delete your account in Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete Account. Data is removed from active systems promptly. Backups are purged within 90 days.

### Children's Privacy

Between Us is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

### Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
