## The Core Feeling

Between Us is a **quiet place** two people share. It's not a tool,
not a tracker, not a coach. It's the digital equivalent of lying in bed
together, phones down, just‚Ä¶ talking. Every word we write should feel
like it belongs in that space.

**Brand line:** *Where closeness deepens.*

## Between Us ALWAYS‚Ä¶

- Uses **first names or "love"** ‚Äî never "User" or "you"
- Treats the **couple as equals** ‚Äî partner's premium unlocks for both
- Puts **privacy front and center** ‚Äî encrypted by default, no cloud without consent
- Feels **handcrafted** ‚Äî like someone cared about every word
- Respects **silence** ‚Äî not every screen needs a subtitle
- Celebrates **small moments** ‚Äî a quiet journal entry matters as much as a milestone

## Between Us NEVER‚Ä¶

- Uses **gamification language**: streak, points, score, level up, achievement unlocked, leaderboard, challenge, badge, rank
- Uses **clinical/analytical language**: relationship health, engagement score, churn, retention, conversion, funnel, optimization
- **Shames inactivity**: "You haven't journaled in 3 days!" ‚Üí Instead, stay quiet or say "Welcome back üíú"
- Uses **ALL CAPS** for excitement (except subtle eyebrow labels like "TODAY'S PROMPT")
- Shows **countdown timers** or urgency tactics for purchases
- Uses **manipulative dark patterns**: "Are you sure you want to miss out?"
- Sends **guilt-based notifications**: "Your partner is waiting‚Ä¶" with a red badge
- **Compares couples**: no rankings, no "other couples also‚Ä¶", no benchmarks
- Uses the word **"user"** in any visible UI

## Visual Tone ‚Äî Midnight Intimacy Palette

- **Dark theme only** ‚Äî Ink-black (#070509) background, soft cream text (#F2E9E6)
- **Charcoal-plum surfaces** (#131016) ‚Äî cards, inputs, elevated UI elements
- **Surface-plum** (#1C1520) ‚Äî secondary surfaces, subtle elevation
- **Surface-elevated** (#241C28) ‚Äî tertiary surfaces, modal overlays
- **Wine accent** (#7A1E4E) ‚Äî primary CTAs, active states, key interactions
- **Mulberry accent** (#9A2E5E) ‚Äî secondary accent, links, subtle highlights
- **Wine-deep** (#4C1030) ‚Äî gradient endpoints, deep accent
- **Matte gold** (#A89060) ‚Äî **premium-only**, never in main UI. Reserved for Pro badge, paywall, milestones.
- **Plum vignette** (#1A0D18) ‚Äî atmospheric gradient backgrounds
- **No teal, no green, no bright pink** ‚Äî the palette is plum, wine, and cream only
- **Glass/blur effects** ‚Äî plum-tinted glass `rgba(28,21,32,...)`, everything feels layered and soft
- **Borders** ‚Äî white at very low opacity: `rgba(255,255,255,0.06)` ‚Äî never raw white, never colored
- **No hard borders** ‚Äî rounded corners (cards: 20, buttons: 16, pills: 999), subtle gradients, breathing space
- **Shadows**: Dramatically reduced ‚Äî max opacity 0.12, mostly 0.04‚Äì0.08
- **Animations**: Gentle. Slow fades (250‚Äì1000ms), soft springs (damping 20‚Äì25). Never bouncy or attention-seeking.

## Premium Philosophy

Premium in Between Us is an **invitation to go deeper**, not a wall.
Free users can preview a taste of the experience ‚Äî 3 read-only prompts across
heat levels 1-3. Premium unlocks everything: unlimited prompts, responses,
love notes, calendar, partner connection, cloud sync, and ongoing new content.

**Never say:**
- "You've reached your limit"
- "Upgrade to continue"
- "You can't do that"

**Instead say:**
- "Discover the full experience"
- "There's more waiting for you"
- "This is part of the deeper journey"
- "Premium unlocks responding, saving, sharing, and everything else"

---

## Notification Philosophy

Notifications are a privilege, not a right. Every notification must pass
this test: **"Would I want to receive this while lying next to my partner?"**

If the answer is no, don't send it.

- Maximum 1 notification per day
- Never guilt-based
- Always dismissable
- Never reveal private content in the preview
